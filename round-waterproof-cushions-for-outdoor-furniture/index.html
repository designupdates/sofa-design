Round waterproof cushions are designed to effectively repel water, ensuring they remain dry and comfortable even when exposed to rain or accidental spills. In addition to the outer fabric, waterproof cushions often incorporate an inner layer of waterproof coating. This coating acts as an additional barrier against moisture, ensuring that even if the outer fabric becomes wet, the cushion's interior remains dry.

The water-resistant properties of round waterproof cushions make them an excellent choice for outdoor furniture in areas with unpredictable weather or frequent spills.
