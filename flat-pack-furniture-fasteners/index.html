Flat Pack Furniture Fasteners: A Guide to Choosing the Right One for Your Project

Written by Luffy Aug 01, 2027 · 17 min read

Flat pack furniture is a great way to save money and space, but it can be a pain to assemble. The right fasteners can make all the difference, helping you get the job done quickly and easily. In this article, we'll discuss the different types of flat pack furniture fasteners available and help you choose the right one for your project.

There are two main types of flat pack furniture fasteners: cam locks and dowels. Cam locks are the most common type of fastener, and they're easy to use. They consist of two pieces: a male cam and a female cam. The male cam is inserted into the female cam and then turned to lock the two pieces together. Dowels are also easy to use, but they're not as strong as cam locks. They consist of a cylindrical piece of wood or metal that is inserted into a hole in the furniture. The dowel is then glued in place to hold the pieces together.

Now that you know about the different types of flat pack furniture fasteners, you can choose the right one for your project. If you're assembling furniture that will be used frequently, such as a bed or a dresser, you'll want to use cam locks. If you're assembling furniture that will not be used as often, such as a bookcase or a nightstand, you can use dowels.
