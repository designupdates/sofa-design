Coastal-inspired designs

Cornwall's captivating coastline serves as an endless source of inspiration for garden furniture designers, resulting in pieces that evoke the charm and tranquility of the seaside.

  • Seaside hues:

    Shades of blue, green, and white dominate coastal-inspired designs, reminiscent of the ocean, sky, and sandy beaches.

  • Natural materials:

    Rattan, wicker, and driftwood are commonly used materials, echoing the organic beauty of the Cornish coastline.

  • Coastal motifs:

    Designs often incorporate seashells, starfish, and other marine elements, adding a touch of whimsy and coastal character.

  • Relaxed elegance:

Sustainable materials

Cornwall's garden furniture makers are increasingly embracing sustainable materials, driven by a desire to protect the environment and create furniture that is both stylish and eco-friendly.

1. Reclaimed and recycled materials:
Many artisans incorporate reclaimed wood, metal, and plastic into their designs, giving new life to discarded materials. This not only reduces waste but also adds a unique character to each piece.

2. Natural and renewable materials:
Rattan, bamboo, and water hyacinth are popular choices for sustainable garden furniture. These materials are rapidly renewable, making them a more environmentally friendly option compared to traditional materials like teak or mahogany.

3. Eco-friendly finishes:
Furniture makers are also opting for environmentally friendly finishes, such as water-based stains and paints, which have a lower environmental impact than traditional solvent-based finishes.

4. Durability and longevity:
Sustainable materials are often more durable and long-lasting than conventional materials, reducing the need for frequent replacements and minimizing waste.

By choosing garden furniture made from sustainable materials, you can enjoy the beauty of your outdoor space while contributing to a greener and more sustainable future.

Weather-resistant options

Cornwall's coastal location and unpredictable weather conditions demand garden furniture that can withstand the elements. Cornish furniture makers offer a wide range of weather-resistant options to ensure that your outdoor furniture remains beautiful and functional for years to come.

1. Teak:
Teak is a naturally weather-resistant hardwood that is highly durable and long-lasting. It is resistant to rot, decay, and insect infestation, making it an ideal choice for outdoor furniture in Cornwall. Teak furniture develops a silvery-gray patina over time, which adds to its rustic charm.

2. Stainless steel:
Stainless steel is another excellent choice for weather-resistant garden furniture. It is highly durable, rust-resistant, and easy to clean. Stainless steel furniture is also lightweight and stackable, making it easy to store when not in use.

3. Powder-coated aluminum:
Powder-coated aluminum is a popular choice for contemporary garden furniture. It is lightweight, durable, and resistant to rust and corrosion. Powder coating provides an additional layer of protection against the elements, making it a great option for coastal areas.

4. Synthetic materials:
Synthetic materials, such as polyethylene and polypropylene, are also commonly used for weather-resistant garden furniture. These materials are UV-resistant, fade-resistant, and easy to clean. Synthetic furniture is also lightweight and low-maintenance, making it a practical choice for busy homeowners.

When choosing weather-resistant garden furniture from Cornwall, consider the specific materials and features that best suit your needs and preferences. With so many options available, you can find pieces that will provide years of enjoyment, regardless of the weather.

FAQ

Question 1: How do I choose the right garden furniture for my space?
Answer 1: Consider the size and layout of your garden, as well as your personal style and needs. Choose furniture that is proportional to the space and that complements your existing dÃ©cor.

Question 2: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer 2: There are many durable and weather-resistant materials to choose from, including teak, stainless steel, powder-coated aluminum, and synthetic materials like polyethylene. Consider the climate in your area and the level of maintenance you are willing to undertake.

Question 3: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 3: Regular cleaning and maintenance are key to extending the life of your garden furniture. Clean your furniture with a mild detergent and water, and apply a protective sealant or oil as recommended by the manufacturer.

Question 4: How can I make my garden furniture more comfortable?
Answer 4: Add comfortable cushions and pillows to your seating, and consider using an outdoor rug to define the space and add warmth. You can also incorporate outdoor lighting to create a cozy and inviting atmosphere.

Question 5: How can I use garden furniture to create different zones in my garden?
Answer 5: Arrange your furniture in different areas of your garden to create distinct zones for dining, lounging, and relaxation. Use dividers, such as potted plants or screens, to define the spaces and create a sense of privacy.

Question 6: How can I store my garden furniture during the off-season?
Answer 6: If you live in an area with harsh winters or prolonged periods of bad weather, it's best to store your garden furniture in a dry and protected location. Clean and dry your furniture thoroughly before storing, and consider using furniture covers to provide additional protection.

Question 7: Where can I find high-quality garden furniture in Cornwall?
