Answer 2: When choosing the right size garden furniture, you need to consider the size of your garden and the number of people you typically entertain. If you plan to use it for relaxing, you may want to choose a comfortable lounge chair or sofa.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?

Answer 3: The best way to care for your garden furniture is to clean it regularly. You can use a mild detergent and water to clean most types of garden furniture. You may also want to apply a sealant or protector to help protect the furniture from the elements.

Question 4: How can I make my garden furniture more comfortable?

Answer 4: There are a few things you can do to make your garden furniture more comfortable. You can add cushions and pillows to your chairs and sofas.
