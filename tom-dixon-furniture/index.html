Born in Tunisia and raised in England, Dixon's path to furniture design was unconventional. After studying art and design, he embarked on a career in music, playing bass guitar in various bands. Dixon's designs are characterized by their bold aesthetics, innovative use of materials, and meticulous attention to detail.
