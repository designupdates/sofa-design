Protects against moisture

Ronseal Furniture Oil provides exceptional protection against moisture, safeguarding your wooden furniture from warping, swelling, and rotting. Its advanced formula penetrates deeply into the wood, creating a water-resistant barrier that repels spills, condensation, and humidity.

Untreated wood can absorb moisture, leading to swelling, warping, and even mold growth. Ronseal Furniture Oil effectively prevents moisture absorption, ensuring that your furniture remains intact and beautiful.

The water-resistant barrier formed by Ronseal Furniture Oil also protects against water rings and stains. Spills can be easily wiped away without leaving any damage, preserving the pristine appearance of your furniture.

Regular application of Ronseal Furniture Oil is essential for maintaining its protective properties. By following the recommended application instructions, you can ensure that your wooden furniture is shielded from moisture and its damaging effects.

FAQ

Have questions about using Ronseal Furniture Oil on garden furniture? Here are some frequently asked questions and answers to help you get started:

Question 1: Can I use Ronseal Furniture Oil on outdoor furniture?
Answer: Absolutely! Ronseal Furniture Oil is suitable for both indoor and outdoor use. It provides excellent protection against the elements, including rain, sun, and wind.

Question 2: How often should I apply Ronseal Furniture Oil to my garden furniture?
Answer: For optimal protection, it's recommended to apply Ronseal Furniture Oil to your garden furniture every 6-12 months, or more frequently if exposed to harsh weather conditions.

Question 3: Can I use Ronseal Furniture Oil on all types of garden furniture?
Answer: Ronseal Furniture Oil is suitable for use on most types of garden furniture, including teak, cedar, pine, and metal. However, it's always best to test it in an inconspicuous area first.

Question 4: How do I prepare my garden furniture before applying Ronseal Furniture Oil?
Answer: To ensure proper absorption and protection, clean your garden furniture thoroughly with a mild detergent and water. Allow it to dry completely before applying Ronseal Furniture Oil.

Question 5: Can I apply Ronseal Furniture Oil over existing finishes?
Answer: Yes, Ronseal Furniture Oil can be applied over existing finishes. However, it's important to note that it may alter the appearance of the finish. It's best to test it in an inconspicuous area first.

Question 6: How long does Ronseal Furniture Oil take to dry?

Question 7: Can I use Ronseal Furniture Oil on painted garden furniture?
