Easy to clean and maintain.

Cat litter tray furniture is generally easy to clean and maintain. Here are some tips:

Empty the litter box daily: This is the most important thing you can do to keep your cat litter tray furniture clean and fresh. Remove all of the solid waste and shake the litter to loosen any clumps. You may also want to use a scoop with a sifting grate to remove any fine particles of waste.

Wash the litter box regularly: Once a week, or more often if needed, wash the litter box with warm soapy water. Be sure to rinse the box thoroughly and allow it to dry completely before refilling it with litter.

Clean the furniture: The outside of the furniture can be cleaned with a damp cloth or a mild detergent. Be sure to avoid using harsh chemicals, as these can damage the finish.

Replace the litter: The type of litter you use will also affect how often you need to clean the furniture. Some litters are more absorbent than others, and some are better at controlling odor. Experiment with different types of litter to find one that works best for you and your cat.

By following these tips, you can keep your cat litter tray furniture clean and fresh, and your cat will be happy to use it.
