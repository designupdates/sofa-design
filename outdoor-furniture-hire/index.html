FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've compiled a list of frequently asked questions to help you make informed decisions and get the most out of your garden furniture hire experience.

Question 1: What types of garden furniture are available for hire?
Answer 1: Garden furniture hire companies offer a wide range of options to suit different needs and preferences. Common types of garden furniture available for hire include lounge sets, dining sets, chairs, tables, umbrellas, and ottomans. Some companies also offer specialized furniture pieces, such as hammocks, swings, and fire pits. Question 2: How do I choose the right garden furniture for my needs?
Answer 2: Consider the following factors when choosing garden furniture: the size and layout of your outdoor space, the purpose of the furniture (e.g., dining, lounging, relaxation), the number of people you typically entertain, your personal style and preferences, and your budget. Question 3: Can I mix and match different pieces of garden furniture?
Answer 3: Yes, mixing and matching different pieces of garden furniture is a great way to create a unique and personalized outdoor space. Many hire companies offer a variety of styles and designs to choose from, allowing you to mix and match pieces to achieve the desired look and functionality. Question 4: How long can I rent garden furniture for?
Answer 4: The rental period for garden furniture varies depending on the hire company and the specific furniture items. Some companies offer short-term rentals (e.g., for a weekend or a party) while others offer long-term rentals (e.g., for the entire summer season). Be sure to inquire about the rental period when booking your furniture. Question 5: What is the cost of renting garden furniture?
Answer 5: The cost of renting garden furniture varies depending on the type of furniture, the rental period, and the hire company. Generally, the larger and more elaborate the furniture, the higher the rental cost. It's a good idea to compare prices from different hire companies to find the best deal. Question 6: What is the best way to care for rented garden furniture?
Answer 6: To properly care for rented garden furniture, follow these tips: clean the furniture regularly with a mild detergent and water, avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials, protect the furniture from direct sunlight and extreme weather conditions when not in use, and store the furniture properly during the off-season.

We hope these FAQs have provided you with valuable information about garden furniture hire. If you have any further questions, don't hesitate to contact the hire company directly.
