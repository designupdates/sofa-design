Rattan Garden Furniture Covers: The Ultimate Guide to Protection and Style

Written by Eveline Aug 25, 2025 · 12 min read

Whether it's a cozy patio set or an elegant outdoor lounge, rattan garden furniture adds a touch of bohemian charm to any outdoor space. However, exposure to the elements can take a toll on these delicate pieces. Enter rattan garden furniture covers – the secret to preserving the beauty and longevity of your beloved furniture.

This comprehensive guide will delve into the world of rattan garden furniture covers, exploring their materials, designs, and most importantly, how to find the perfect cover for your specific needs. So, let's dive in and ensure that your rattan furniture remains a cherished addition to your outdoor oasis for years to come.

Before we dive into the specifics of rattan garden furniture covers, let's first understand what rattan is and why it requires special protection.

Rattan Garden Furniture Covers

Here are seven important points to consider when choosing a rattan garden furniture cover:

  • Waterproof
  • UV-resistant
  • Breathable
  • Durable
  • Easy to clean
  • Customizable
  • Aesthetically pleasing

By keeping these points in mind, you can find the perfect cover to protect your rattan garden furniture from the elements and keep it looking its best for years to come.

UV-resistant

UV resistance is crucial for rattan garden furniture covers as exposure to the sun's harmful UV rays can cause the rattan to fade, become brittle, and crack over time. A UV-resistant cover will protect your furniture from these damaging rays, ensuring that it retains its beautiful appearance and structural integrity for longer.

  • Blocks harmful UV rays: UV-resistant covers are specially treated to block out the sun's damaging UV rays, preventing them from penetrating the fabric and reaching the rattan furniture beneath.
  • Prevents fading: UV rays can cause rattan to fade and lose its natural color. A UV-resistant cover will help to preserve the original color of your furniture, keeping it looking vibrant and new for longer.
  • Protects against brittleness: UV rays can make rattan brittle and more susceptible to breakage. A UV-resistant cover will help to keep your furniture flexible and strong, reducing the risk of damage.
  • Extends the lifespan of your furniture: By protecting your rattan furniture from UV damage, a UV-resistant cover will help to extend its lifespan and keep it looking its best for many years to come.

When choosing a rattan garden furniture cover, be sure to look for one that is specifically labeled as UV-resistant. This will ensure that your furniture is fully protected from the sun's harmful rays.

Breathable

Breathability is an important consideration for rattan garden furniture covers as it allows air to circulate and prevents moisture from becoming trapped inside the cover. This is especially important in humid climates or during the summer months when condensation can form under the cover. A breathable cover will help to prevent mold and mildew from growing on your furniture, which can cause damage and discoloration.

  • Prevents moisture buildup: A breathable cover allows air to circulate, preventing moisture from becoming trapped inside the cover and causing condensation. This helps to keep your furniture dry and free from mold and mildew.
  • Reduces heat buildup: A breathable cover allows heat to escape, preventing your furniture from overheating in the sun. This is especially important for dark-colored furniture, which can absorb more heat.
  • Extends the lifespan of your furniture: By preventing moisture and heat buildup, a breathable cover will help to extend the lifespan of your rattan garden furniture and keep it looking its best for longer.
  • Easy to use: Breathable covers are easy to put on and take off, making it convenient to protect your furniture when needed.

When choosing a rattan garden furniture cover, look for one that is made from a breathable material, such as polyester or canvas. These materials allow air to circulate while still providing protection from the elements.

Durable

Durability is an essential factor to consider when choosing a rattan garden furniture cover, especially if you live in an area with harsh weather conditions. A durable cover will be able to withstand the elements and protect your furniture for years to come.

Here are some factors that contribute to the durability of a rattan garden furniture cover:

  • Material: The material of the cover is a key factor in determining its durability. Look for covers made from high-quality materials, such as polyester or canvas. These materials are resistant to fading, tearing, and mildew.
  • Construction: The construction of the cover is also important. Look for covers that are well-made with strong seams and reinforced corners. These features will help to prevent the cover from tearing or ripping.
  • Fit: A well-fitting cover will protect your furniture more effectively and will be less likely to be damaged by wind or rain. Make sure to measure your furniture before purchasing a cover to ensure a snug fit.
  • Warranty: A good warranty is a sign that the manufacturer is confident in the durability of their product. Look for covers with a warranty of at least one year.

By choosing a durable rattan garden furniture cover, you can protect your furniture from the elements and ensure that it lasts for many years to come.

Easy to clean

An easy-to-clean rattan garden furniture cover is essential for keeping your furniture looking its best. After all, no one wants to spend hours scrubbing a dirty cover! Here are a few things to look for when choosing an easy-to-clean cover:

  • Machine-washable: A machine-washable cover is the easiest to clean. Simply throw it in the washing machine on a gentle cycle and let it air dry.
  • Wipe-clean: A wipe-clean cover can be cleaned with a damp cloth or sponge. This is a good option if you don't want to remove the cover from your furniture every time it needs to be cleaned.
  • Fade-resistant: A fade-resistant cover will not lose its color after being washed or exposed to the sun. This will help to keep your cover looking new for longer.
  • Mildew-resistant: A mildew-resistant cover will not grow mold or mildew, even in humid conditions. This is important for keeping your furniture clean and free of odors.

By choosing an easy-to-clean rattan garden furniture cover, you can keep your furniture looking its best with minimal effort.

Customizable

A customizable rattan garden furniture cover allows you to choose the perfect fit and style for your furniture. Here are a few things to consider when choosing a customizable cover:

  • Size: Make sure to measure your furniture before ordering a cover. A custom cover will be made to the exact size of your furniture, ensuring a snug fit.
  • Shape: Rattan garden furniture comes in a variety of shapes and sizes. A customizable cover can be made to fit any shape, including round, square, rectangular, and L-shaped furniture.
  • Color: Choose a color that complements your furniture and outdoor décor. Custom covers are available in a wide range of colors, so you're sure to find one that matches your taste.
  • Features: Some customizable covers offer additional features, such as vents, zippers, and pockets. These features can add convenience and functionality to your cover.

By choosing a customizable rattan garden furniture cover, you can create a cover that is perfectly suited to your needs.

Aesthetically pleasing

In addition to being functional, a rattan garden furniture cover should also be aesthetically pleasing. After all, you want your cover to complement your furniture and outdoor décor. Here are a few things to consider when choosing an aesthetically pleasing cover:

  • Color: Choose a color that complements your furniture and outdoor décor. Custom covers are available in a wide range of colors, so you're sure to find one that matches your taste.
  • Style: Choose a style that matches your furniture and personal taste. Some covers are available with decorative features, such as piping, cording, and tassels.
  • Texture: Choose a texture that is visually appealing and complements your furniture. Some covers are available in a variety of textures, such as smooth, textured, and quilted.
  • Fit: A well-fitting cover will look more aesthetically pleasing than a cover that is too loose or too tight. Make sure to measure your furniture before ordering a cover to ensure a snug fit.

By choosing an aesthetically pleasing rattan garden furniture cover, you can create a cover that is both functional and stylish.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture:

Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer: The best material for garden furniture depends on your individual needs and preferences. Some popular materials include teak, cedar, redwood, and wrought iron. Teak is a durable hardwood that is naturally resistant to rot and decay. Cedar is another durable wood that is also resistant to insects. Redwood is a strong and lightweight wood that is also resistant to rot and decay. Wrought iron is a durable metal that is resistant to rust and corrosion.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: When choosing the right size garden furniture, it is important to consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. You should also consider the size of your furniture in relation to the other elements in your outdoor space, such as your deck or patio.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to care for your garden furniture is to clean it regularly and protect it from the elements. You should clean your furniture with a mild soap and water solution. You can also use a commercial furniture cleaner. To protect your furniture from the elements, you should cover it with a furniture cover when it is not in use.

Question 4: How do I store my garden furniture during the winter?
Answer: If you live in an area with cold winters, you should store your garden furniture indoors during the winter months. You can store your furniture in a garage, shed, or basement. If you do not have a place to store your furniture indoors, you should cover it with a furniture cover.

Question 5: How do I repair my garden furniture?
Answer: If your garden furniture is damaged, you can repair it yourself using a variety of methods. You can use wood glue to repair cracks in wood furniture. You can also use metal glue to repair cracks in metal furniture. If your furniture is made of fabric, you can repair it using a sewing machine.

Question 6: How do I refinish my garden furniture?
Answer: If your garden furniture is looking old and worn, you can refinish it to give it a new look. You can refinish your furniture using a variety of methods, such as painting, staining, or varnishing.

These are just a few of the most frequently asked questions about garden furniture. If you have any other questions, please feel free to contact a garden furniture expert.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you choose the perfect garden furniture for your outdoor space:

Tip 1: Consider your needs. Before you start shopping for garden furniture, take some time to think about your needs. How will you be using the furniture? Will you be entertaining guests? Do you need furniture that is comfortable for lounging? Once you know your needs, you can start to narrow down your choices.

Tip 2: Choose the right material. Garden furniture is made from a variety of materials, including wood, metal, plastic, and wicker. Each material has its own advantages and disadvantages. Wood is durable and stylish, but it requires regular maintenance. Metal is also durable, but it can be heavy and hot to the touch. Plastic is lightweight and weather-resistant, but it can be less durable than other materials. Wicker is lightweight and comfortable, but it can be damaged by moisture.

Tip 3: Consider your style. Garden furniture comes in a variety of styles, from traditional to contemporary. Choose a style that complements your home and outdoor décor. If you have a traditional home, you may want to choose furniture made from wood or metal. If you have a contemporary home, you may want to choose furniture made from plastic or wicker.

Tip 4: Measure your space. Before you buy garden furniture, be sure to measure your outdoor space. This will help you choose furniture that is the right size for your space. You don't want to buy furniture that is too large or too small for your space.
