Ercol Style Coffee Tables: A Timeless Classic

Written by Adams Apr 14, 2027 · 10 min read

Ercol style coffee tables are a timeless classic, renowned for their elegant design and exceptional craftsmanship. These coffee tables are the perfect addition to any living room, adding a touch of sophistication and warmth to the space.

Ercol was founded in 1920 by Lucian Ercolani, and was known for its use of innovative techniques and high-quality materials. The company's coffee tables are a testament to their commitment to quality, each piece carefully crafted from solid wood and featuring meticulous attention to detail.

In the following sections, we will delve into the key characteristics of Ercol style coffee tables, explore the various designs available, and provide tips on how to choose the perfect coffee table for your home.

ercol style coffee table

Ercol style coffee tables are known for their exceptional quality, timeless design, and versatility. Here are six key points to note about these coffee tables:

  • Solid wood construction
  • Meticulous craftsmanship
  • Elegant and timeless design
  • Versatile and functional
  • Available in a range of styles
  • Durable and long-lasting

Whether you are looking for a classic or contemporary coffee table, Ercol style coffee tables offer a perfect blend of style and functionality.

Solid wood construction

Ercol style coffee tables are renowned for their solid wood construction, which contributes to their exceptional durability and longevity.

  • Beechwood: Ercol primarily uses beechwood for its coffee tables, known for its strength, hardness, and beautiful grain pattern.
  • Elmwood: Some Ercol coffee tables are also crafted from elmwood, which is another durable and attractive hardwood.
  • Oakwood: Occasionally, Ercol uses oakwood for its coffee tables, known for its exceptional strength and resistance to wear and tear.
  • Expert craftsmanship: Ercol's skilled craftsmen carefully select and join the solid wood pieces, ensuring a sturdy and durable construction.

The solid wood construction not only enhances the durability of Ercol coffee tables but also adds to their timeless appeal, making them a cherished piece of furniture for generations to come.

Meticulous craftsmanship

Ercol style coffee tables are not just known for their solid wood construction but also for their meticulous craftsmanship, which is evident in every detail of these tables.

Each Ercol coffee table is carefully handcrafted by skilled artisans who take pride in their work. They use traditional techniques and pay close attention to every step of the construction process, from selecting the finest wood to applying the finishing touches.

The joints are precisely cut and fitted, ensuring a sturdy and durable construction. The surfaces are sanded to a smooth finish, showcasing the natural beauty of the wood grain. The application of a protective finish enhances the durability of the table and brings out the rich color of the wood.

The meticulous craftsmanship of Ercol style coffee tables not only adds to their aesthetic appeal but also ensures their longevity, making them a cherished piece of furniture that can be enjoyed for generations to come.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture to help you make informed choices for your outdoor space:

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer: The best materials for garden furniture are those that can withstand the elements, such as teak, cedar, and acacia. These woods are naturally resistant to rot, decay, and insects, making them durable and long-lasting.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: Consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. Choose furniture that is proportionate to the space and allows for comfortable movement.

Question 3: What style of garden furniture should I choose?
Answer: The style of garden furniture you choose should complement your home's architecture and your personal taste. Consider classic, contemporary, or a mix of styles to create a unique outdoor space.

Question 4: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer: Regular cleaning and maintenance will help extend the life of your garden furniture. Clean it with a mild soap solution and protect it with a sealant or oil to preserve its finish.

Question 5: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer: If your garden furniture is made from durable materials like teak or cedar, it can withstand being left outside all year round. However, it's best to cover it during harsh weather conditions to protect it from excessive sun, rain, or snow.

Question 6: Where can I buy high-quality garden furniture?
Answer: You can find high-quality garden furniture at reputable furniture stores, home improvement centers, and online retailers. Look for brands that specialize in outdoor furniture and offer a warranty on their products.

Closing Paragraph for FAQ:

By considering these factors, you can choose the perfect garden furniture to enhance your outdoor space and create a comfortable and stylish haven for relaxation and entertainment.

Tips

Here are four practical tips to help you choose and care for your garden furniture:

Tip 1: Consider your climate and usage. Choose furniture made from materials that are suitable for your climate and how you plan to use it. For example, if you live in a rainy area, opt for furniture that is water-resistant or quick-drying.

Tip 2: Measure your space before you buy. Make sure to measure your outdoor space and plan out where you want to place your furniture before making a purchase. This will help you avoid buying pieces that are too large or too small for your space.

Tip 3: Choose comfortable and stylish furniture. Garden furniture should be both comfortable and stylish. Look for pieces that are ergonomically designed and made from materials that are soft and inviting to the touch.

Tip 4: Protect your furniture from the elements. To extend the life of your garden furniture, protect it from the elements by covering it when not in use and storing it in a dry place during harsh weather conditions.

Closing Paragraph for Tips:

By following these tips, you can choose and care for your garden furniture to create a comfortable and stylish outdoor space that you can enjoy for years to come.

Conclusion

Garden furniture is an essential element for creating a comfortable and stylish outdoor space. By choosing the right materials, size, style, and care routine, you can find garden furniture that perfectly complements your home and provides years of enjoyment.

Remember, the best garden furniture is made from durable materials, such as teak, cedar, or acacia, and is designed to withstand the elements. Consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain when choosing the size of your furniture. The style of your garden furniture should complement your home's architecture and your personal taste.

With proper care and maintenance, your garden furniture will last for many years. Clean it regularly with a mild soap solution and protect it with a sealant or oil to preserve its finish. Cover it during harsh weather conditions to protect it from excessive sun, rain, or snow.

By following these tips, you can choose and care for your garden furniture to create a beautiful and inviting outdoor space that you and your family can enjoy for years to come.
