Answer 3: When choosing garden furniture, it is important to consider your needs and preferences. Think about how you will be using the furniture and what style you prefer. You should also consider the size of your garden and the amount of space you have available.

Answer 4: The best way to care for your garden furniture is to clean it regularly and protect it from the elements. Here are a few tips to help you choose the perfect garden furniture for your home:
