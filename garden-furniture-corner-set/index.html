Garden Furniture Corner Set: A Guide to Finding the Perfect Set for Your Outdoor Oasis

Written by Petter Aug 10, 2028 · 13 min read

Creating a stylish and inviting outdoor space is a dream for many homeowners. A garden furniture corner set is an essential element in completing this vision, providing a comfortable and stylish seating area for you and your guests to relax and enjoy the fresh air.

With so many different corner sets available, choosing the right one for your needs can be a daunting task. This guide will provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision, from the different materials and styles available to the features to look for when making your purchase.

Now that you have a better understanding of what to look for in a garden furniture corner set, let's take a closer look at the different materials and styles available.

garden furniture corner set

When choosing a garden furniture corner set, there are a few important points to keep in mind:

  • Material: Choose a material that is durable and weather-resistant, such as teak, acacia, or aluminum.
  • Style: Select a style that complements your outdoor décor, from traditional to modern.
  • Size: Make sure the set is the right size for your space, both in terms of seating capacity and overall dimensions.
  • Comfort: Look for a set with comfortable cushions and supportive seating.
  • Functionality: Consider sets with additional features, such as adjustable tables or built-in storage.
  • Price: Set a budget and stick to it, keeping in mind that quality materials and craftsmanship come at a higher cost.
  • Maintenance: Choose a set that is easy to clean and maintain, especially if it will be exposed to the elements.

By following these tips, you can find the perfect garden furniture corner set to create a stylish and inviting outdoor oasis.

Material: Choose a material that is durable and weather-resistant, such as teak, acacia, or aluminum.

When choosing a garden furniture corner set, the material you select will play a significant role in its durability and weather resistance. Consider the following materials:

Teak: Teak is a naturally weather-resistant hardwood that is known for its durability and longevity. It is a popular choice for outdoor furniture because it can withstand exposure to sun, rain, and wind. Teak furniture will develop a beautiful silver-gray patina over time, which adds to its charm and character.

Acacia: Acacia is another durable hardwood that is often used in outdoor furniture. It is not as weather-resistant as teak, but it is still a good choice for areas with moderate climates. Acacia furniture is typically less expensive than teak furniture.

Aluminum: Aluminum is a lightweight and durable metal that is resistant to rust and corrosion. It is a good choice for outdoor furniture because it can withstand the elements and is easy to clean. Aluminum furniture is typically less expensive than teak or acacia furniture.

Ultimately, the best material for your garden furniture corner set will depend on your specific needs and budget. If you live in a harsh climate, teak or acacia would be a good choice. If you are on a budget, aluminum would be a good option.

In addition to the material, you should also consider the finish of your garden furniture corner set. A high-quality finish will help to protect the furniture from the elements and extend its lifespan. Look for furniture with a powder-coated finish or a marine-grade finish, which are both designed to withstand the rigors of outdoor use.

Style: Select a style that complements your outdoor décor, from traditional to modern.

The style of your garden furniture corner set should complement your outdoor décor. Consider the overall style of your home and garden, and choose a set that will fit in well with the existing aesthetic.

  • Traditional: Traditional garden furniture is typically made from wood or wrought iron and features classic designs. This style is perfect for homes with a traditional or colonial architectural style.
  • Modern: Modern garden furniture is typically made from metal, plastic, or concrete and features clean lines and simple designs. This style is perfect for homes with a modern or contemporary architectural style.
  • Transitional: Transitional garden furniture combines elements of both traditional and modern styles. This style is perfect for homes that have a mix of traditional and modern elements.
  • Tropical: Tropical garden furniture is typically made from natural materials, such as bamboo or rattan, and features bright colors and patterns. This style is perfect for homes in warm climates or for those who want to create a tropical oasis in their backyard.

Once you have considered the overall style of your home and garden, you can start to narrow down your choices. Think about the colors and patterns that you want to incorporate into your outdoor space, and choose a garden furniture corner set that will complement your existing décor.

Size: Make sure the set is the right size for your space, both in terms of seating capacity and overall dimensions.

The size of your garden furniture corner set will depend on the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. Consider the following factors when choosing a size:

  • Seating capacity: How many people do you want to be able to seat comfortably in your corner set? Choose a set that has enough seating for your needs, but not so much that it overwhelms your space.
  • Overall dimensions: Measure the space where you plan to put your corner set to make sure that it will fit. Consider the height, width, and depth of the set, as well as the space you need to move around it.
  • Scale: The size of your corner set should be in proportion to the size of your outdoor space. A large corner set in a small space will look cramped and out of place, while a small corner set in a large space will look lost.
  • Traffic flow: Make sure that your corner set does not block traffic flow in your outdoor space. Leave enough space for people to move around comfortably.

Once you have considered all of these factors, you can start to narrow down your choices. It is always a good idea to measure your space before you purchase a corner set to make sure that it will fit properly.

Comfort: Look for a set with comfortable cushions and supportive seating.

Comfort is key when choosing a garden furniture corner set. After all, you want to be able to relax and enjoy your time outdoors. Consider the following factors when evaluating the comfort of a corner set:

Cushions: The cushions on your corner set should be comfortable and supportive. Look for cushions that are made from high-quality materials, such as Sunbrella fabric, which is resistant to fading and mildew. The cushions should also be thick enough to provide support and comfort, but not so thick that they make it difficult to get in and out of the furniture.

Seating: The seating on your corner set should be supportive and comfortable. Look for seats that are wide and deep enough to provide ample space to relax. The seats should also be made from a comfortable material, such as wicker or textilene.

Ergonomics: The ergonomics of your corner set are also important for comfort. Make sure that the seats are the right height and that the backrest provides good support. The armrests should also be comfortable and supportive.

Overall design: The overall design of your corner set can also affect its comfort. Look for sets that have a comfortable seating position and that are easy to get in and out of.

By considering all of these factors, you can choose a garden furniture corner set that is both comfortable and stylish.

Functionality: Consider sets with additional features, such as adjustable tables or built-in storage.

In addition to comfort and style, you may also want to consider the functionality of your garden furniture corner set. Some sets come with additional features, such as adjustable tables or built-in storage, which can make your outdoor space more enjoyable and convenient.

Adjustable tables: Adjustable tables are a great option for corner sets because they allow you to customize the height of the table to suit your needs. This is especially useful if you use your corner set for both dining and relaxing. Some adjustable tables also have built-in ice buckets or coolers, which can be a great way to keep your drinks cold on a hot day.

Built-in storage: Built-in storage is another great option for corner sets. This can be a great place to store cushions, blankets, or other outdoor accessories. Some corner sets even have built-in planters, which can be a great way to add some greenery to your outdoor space.

Other features: Other features to consider include cup holders, umbrellas, and wheels. Cup holders can be a great way to keep your drinks within reach, while umbrellas can provide shade on a sunny day. Wheels can make it easy to move your corner set around, which can be useful for cleaning or rearranging your outdoor space.

By considering the functionality of your garden furniture corner set, you can choose a set that meets your specific needs and makes your outdoor space more enjoyable.

Price: Set a budget and stick to it, keeping in mind that quality materials and craftsmanship come at a higher cost.

The price of a garden furniture corner set will vary depending on the materials, size, style, and features. It is important to set a budget before you start shopping so that you do not overspend. Keep in mind that quality materials and craftsmanship come at a higher cost. However, it is also important to find a set that fits your needs and that you will enjoy for many years to come.

  • Materials: The materials used to make your corner set will have a significant impact on the price. Teak and acacia are more expensive than aluminum or plastic, but they are also more durable and weather-resistant. If you live in a harsh climate, it is worth investing in a set made from high-quality materials.
  • Size: The size of your corner set will also affect the price. A larger set will typically cost more than a smaller set. Choose a set that is the right size for your space and needs.
  • Style: The style of your corner set will also affect the price. Traditional sets are typically more expensive than modern sets. Choose a set that complements your outdoor décor and personal style.
  • Features: The features of your corner set will also affect the price. Sets with additional features, such as adjustable tables or built-in storage, will typically cost more than sets without these features. Choose a set that has the features that you need and that fit your budget.

By considering all of these factors, you can set a budget for your garden furniture corner set and find a set that meets your needs and that you will enjoy for many years to come.

Maintenance: Choose a set that is easy to clean and maintain, especially if it will be exposed to the elements.

If your garden furniture corner set will be exposed to the elements, it is important to choose a set that is easy to clean and maintain. This will help to extend the life of your furniture and keep it looking its best.

Here are some factors to consider when choosing a low-maintenance garden furniture corner set:

Materials: Some materials are more low-maintenance than others. For example, teak and aluminum are both naturally weather-resistant and require minimal maintenance. Plastic and wicker are also relatively low-maintenance, but they may require more frequent cleaning.

Cushions: If your corner set has cushions, choose cushions that are made from a durable, weather-resistant fabric. Sunbrella fabric is a good option because it is resistant to fading, mildew, and water.

Overall design: The overall design of your corner set can also affect its maintenance. For example, sets with open frames are easier to clean than sets with solid panels. Sets with removable cushions are also easier to clean than sets with fixed cushions.

By considering all of these factors, you can choose a garden furniture corner set that is easy to clean and maintain, and that will last for many years to come.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture corner sets:

Question 1: How do I choose the right size corner set for my space?
Answer 1: Measure the space where you plan to put your corner set to make sure that it will fit. Consider the height, width, and depth of the set, as well as the space you need to move around it.

Question 2: What materials are best for garden furniture corner sets?
Answer 2: Teak, aluminum, and plastic are all good choices for garden furniture corner sets. Teak is a durable hardwood that is naturally weather- resistant, aluminum is lightweight and rust- resistant, and plastic is affordable and easy to maintain.

Question 3: How do I clean and maintain my garden furniture corner set?
Answer 3: The best way to clean and maintain your garden furniture corner set will depend on the materials used. However, most sets can be cleaned with a mild soap and water solution. Cushions should be regularly brushed or washed to remove dirt and dust.

Question 4: How long will my garden furniture corner set last?
Answer 4: The longevity of your garden furniture corner set will depend on the materials used, the climate in which you live, and how well you maintain it. However, with proper care, most sets can last for many years.

Question 5: Can I leave my garden furniture corner set outside all year round?
Answer 5: Whether or not you can leave your garden furniture corner set outside all year round will depend on the materials used and the climate in which you live. If you live in a harsh climate, it is best to store your set indoors during the winter months.

Question 6: How do I assemble my garden furniture corner set?
Answer 6: Most garden furniture corner sets come with detailed instructions on how to assemble them. However, if you are not comfortable with DIY projects, you can hire a professional to assemble your set for you.

Question 7: Can I buy replacement parts for my garden furniture corner set?
Answer 7: Yes, you can buy replacement parts for most garden furniture corner sets. Contact the manufacturer or retailer of your set to find out if they sell replacement parts.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your garden furniture corner set:

Tip 1: Choose the right location for your set. Your corner set should be placed in a location that is both comfortable and convenient. Consider the amount of sun and shade in the area, as well as the proximity to your home and other amenities.

Tip 2: Protect your set from the elements. If your set will be exposed to the sun, rain, or snow, it is important to protect it from the elements. Use a furniture cover or store your set indoors during inclement weather.

Tip 3: Clean your set regularly. To keep your set looking its best, it is important to clean it regularly. Use a mild soap and water solution to clean the frames and cushions. For more stubborn dirt or stains, you may need to use a stronger cleaner.

Tip 4: Store your set properly during the off-season. If you live in a climate with cold winters, it is important to store your garden furniture corner set properly during the off-season. Clean and dry your set thoroughly, then store it in a dry, protected place.

By following these tips, you can keep your garden furniture corner set looking its best for many years to come.

Conclusion

By taking the time to find the right set, you can create an outdoor oasis that you and your guests will enjoy for many years to come.
