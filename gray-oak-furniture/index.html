FAQ

Need additional information about your new garden furniture? We've compiled a list of frequently asked questions to help you make the most of your outdoor space. Question 1: How do I clean my garden furniture?
Maintaining your garden furniture is essential for extending its lifespan. Use a mild soap solution and warm water to gently clean the surfaces. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners, as they can damage the finish. Rinse thoroughly with clean water and allow to dry completely before use. Question 2: How do I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
To protect your garden furniture from the sun, rain, and other outdoor elements, consider using a furniture cover when it's not in use. Choose a cover made of durable, weather-resistant material to ensure maximum protection. Question 3: How do I store my garden furniture during the winter?
Proper storage is crucial to preserve your garden furniture during the off-season. Clean it thoroughly, allow it to dry completely, and then store it in a dry, well-ventilated area. Avoid storing furniture in direct sunlight or extreme temperatures. Question 4: How do I assemble my garden furniture?
Refer to the assembly instructions provided by the manufacturer. Carefully follow the steps and ensure all parts are securely fastened. Use the appropriate tools and avoid overtightening screws or bolts. Question 5: How do I maintain the finish of my garden furniture?
To maintain the finish of your garden furniture, regular cleaning and protection are key. Use a mild soap solution to clean the surfaces and apply a sealant or protectant as recommended by the manufacturer. This will help preserve the finish and extend the lifespan of your furniture. Question 6: Where can I find replacement parts for my garden furniture?
If you need replacement parts for your garden furniture, contact the manufacturer or retailer where you purchased it. They may have the necessary parts or be able to assist you in obtaining them.
