Durable synthetic rattan

At the heart of Maze Rattan garden furniture lies the exceptional material known as synthetic rattan. This innovative material is meticulously crafted to withstand the rigors of outdoor elements, ensuring lasting beauty and performance.

  • Resistant to fading and cracking:

    Unlike natural rattan, synthetic rattan retains its vibrant color and resists fading caused by prolonged sun exposure. It also exhibits exceptional resistance to cracking, ensuring a pristine appearance for years to come.

  • Withstands extreme temperatures:

    Synthetic rattan furniture can endure both scorching summers and freezing winters without succumbing to warping or damage. Its adaptability to extreme temperatures makes it suitable for a wide range of climates.

  • Water-repellent and quick-drying:

    Rain and spills pose no threat to synthetic rattan. Its water-repellent nature ensures that moisture beads up and rolls off the surface, preventing absorption and damage. Additionally, the material dries quickly, minimizing the risk of mold and mildew growth.

  • UV-protected for lasting color:

    Maze Rattan garden furniture undergoes a rigorous UV treatment process, protecting it from the harmful effects of the sun's ultraviolet rays. This treatment inhibits fading and discoloration, preserving the furniture's vibrant hues season after season.

With its exceptional durability and resistance to various elements, synthetic rattan is the ideal choice for outdoor furniture that combines style with longevity.
