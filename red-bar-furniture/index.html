Question 2: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer: The best material for garden furniture depends on your individual needs and preferences. However, some of the most popular materials for garden furniture include:

  • Teak: Known for its durability and resistance to rot and decay
  • Cedar: Naturally resistant to insects and decay, and has a beautiful reddish hue
  • Wrought iron: Strong and durable, and can be powder-coated for added protection against rust
  • Aluminum: Lightweight and rust-resistant, making it a good choice for coastal areas
  • Plastic: Affordable, lightweight, and easy to maintain, but may not be as durable as other materials

Answer: When choosing garden furniture, it's important to consider the following factors:

  • The size of your garden or patio

  • Your budget

Question 4: How do I care for my garden furniture?
