FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've got answers!

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Garden furniture should be made from durable materials that can withstand the elements. Some popular choices include teak, cedar, and wrought iron.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. You want to choose furniture that is large enough to be comfortable but not so large that it overwhelms your space.

Question 3: What style of garden furniture is right for me?
There are many different styles of garden furniture to choose from, so you can find something that matches your personal taste and the overall aesthetic of your outdoor space.

Question 4: How do I care for my garden furniture?
To keep your garden furniture looking its best, clean it regularly with a mild soap and water solution. You may also want to apply a sealant or stain to protect it from the elements.

Question 5: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Whether or not you can leave your garden furniture outside all year round depends on the climate you live in. If you live in a cold climate, you should store your furniture indoors during the winter months.

Question 6: Where can I buy garden furniture?
You can buy garden furniture at a variety of stores, both online and offline. Some popular places to buy garden furniture include home improvement stores, furniture stores, and online retailers.
