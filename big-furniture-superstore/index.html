Q1. What types of garden furniture are available?

Q2. How can I choose the right garden furniture for my needs?


A2. Consider the size of your outdoor space, the desired level of comfort, the materials that suit your style and the weather conditions in your area.


Q3. What are the advantages of using certain materials like wood, metal, rattan, or plastic?


A3. Wood offers natural beauty and durability, metal provides strength and modern aesthetics, rattan and bamboo are lightweight and eco-friendly, while plastic is low maintenance and water-resistant.


Q4. How can I extend the longevity of my garden furniture?


A4. Regular cleaning, proper storage during harsh weather, using protective covers, and applying appropriate treatments or paints can increase furniture lifespan.

A5. Many retail stores specializing in home and garden products offer a wide selection of garden furniture.


Q6. What factors influence the price range of garden furniture?


A6. Material quality, design, brand reputation, and additional features like cushions or armrests impact the cost of garden furniture.
