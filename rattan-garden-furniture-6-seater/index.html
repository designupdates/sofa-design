FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about rattan garden furniture:

Question 1: What is rattan garden furniture made of?
Answer: Rattan garden furniture is made from a natural material called rattan, which is a type of palm that is native to Southeast Asia. Rattan is a strong and durable material that is resistant to rot and decay, making it ideal for outdoor use.

Question 2: How do I clean rattan garden furniture?
Answer: To clean rattan garden furniture, simply wipe it down with a damp cloth. You can also use a mild soap solution if necessary. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners, as these can damage the furniture.

Question 3: How do I store rattan garden furniture?
Answer: When not in use, rattan garden furniture should be stored in a dry place. If possible, cover the furniture with a tarp or plastic sheeting to protect it from the elements.

Question 4: How long does rattan garden furniture last?
Answer: With proper care and maintenance, rattan garden furniture can last for many years. However, the lifespan of the furniture will vary depending on the climate and how well it is cared for.

Question 5: Is rattan garden furniture comfortable?
Answer: Yes, rattan garden furniture is comfortable. The material is naturally supportive and conforms to the body, providing a comfortable seating experience.

Question 6: Can rattan garden furniture be used indoors?
Answer: Yes, rattan garden furniture can be used indoors. However, it is important to note that the furniture is not as resistant to moisture indoors as it is outdoors. Therefore, it is important to keep the furniture away from sources of moisture, such as humidifiers and fireplaces.
