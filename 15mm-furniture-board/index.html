Tips

Here are some practical tips to help you care for and maintain your 15mm furniture board garden furniture, ensuring its longevity and beauty:

Tip 1: Choose the Right Location:
When placing your garden furniture, select a spot that is not directly exposed to harsh sunlight or excessive moisture. Avoid areas near sprinklers or water features to prevent water damage.

Tip 2: Regular Cleaning:
To keep your garden furniture looking its best, clean it regularly with a mild detergent and water solution. Use a soft cloth or sponge to avoid scratching the surface. Rinse thoroughly and allow the furniture to dry completely before use.

Tip 3: Protect from Harsh Weather:
During periods of inclement weather, such as heavy rain or snowfall, consider covering your garden furniture with a protective cover or moving it indoors if possible. This will help prevent damage from moisture and extreme temperatures.

Tip 4: Inspect and Maintain:
Periodically inspect your garden furniture for any signs of damage, wear, or loose screws. Tighten any loose screws promptly, and address any damage or wear by repairing or replacing the affected parts as needed.
