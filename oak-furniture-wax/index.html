Tips

Here are some practical tips to help you care for your garden furniture and keep it looking its best:

Tip 1: Clean regularly Regular cleaning is essential to remove dirt, debris, and spills that can accumulate on your garden furniture over time. Use a mild soap solution and a soft cloth to gently wipe down your furniture. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners, as these can damage the finish. Tip 2: Protect from the elements If your garden furniture is exposed to the elements, it's important to protect it from the sun, rain, and snow. Apply a UV protectant or sealant to shield it from the sun's harmful rays and prevent fading. Cover your furniture with a tarp or store it in a dry and sheltered area during inclement weather. Tip 3: Oil regularly Oiling your garden furniture helps to nourish the wood and extend its lifespan. Aim to oil your furniture every 3-6 months, or as needed. Use a high-quality oil that is specifically designed for outdoor furniture. Tip 4: Store properly When you're not using your garden furniture, it's important to store it properly to protect it from the elements and pests. Store your furniture in a dry and sheltered area, such as a garage, shed, or covered patio. If possible, elevate your furniture off the ground to prevent moisture from accumulating.

By following these tips, you can help your garden furniture last for many years and continue to enjoy its beauty and functionality for seasons to come.
