FAQ

Welcome to the FAQ section dedicated to garden furniture at Furniture World Redruth Cornwall. Whether you're looking to create a cozy outdoor oasis or a stylish entertaining space, we're here to help you find the perfect pieces and answer any questions you may have.

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer 1: The best materials for garden furniture depend on your specific needs and preferences. Teak, acacia, and eucalyptus are popular choices for their durability and resistance to rot and insects. Metal furniture, such as aluminum or wrought iron, is also durable and easy to maintain. If you prefer a more natural look, consider rattan or wicker furniture, which is made from renewable materials.

Question 2: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 2: To protect your garden furniture from the elements, consider using weatherproof covers or storing it in a dry place during harsh weather conditions. Regular cleaning and maintenance, such as wiping down with a damp cloth and applying protective oils or sealants, can also help extend the life of your furniture.

Question 3: What are some comfortable seating options for my garden?
Answer 3: For comfortable seating in your garden, consider lounge chairs, daybeds, or sectionals with plush cushions. Ottomans and poufs can also provide additional seating and serve as footrests. Choose pieces that are made from water-resistant materials and quick-drying cushions for easy maintenance.

Question 4: How can I create a cohesive look for my garden furniture?
Answer 4: To create a cohesive look for your garden furniture, choose pieces from the same collection or with similar design elements, such as color, material, or style. Incorporate outdoor rugs, cushions, and throws to add pops of color and texture. Consider the overall layout of your garden and arrange the furniture in a way that promotes conversation and easy movement.

Question 5: Where can I find inspiration for designing my garden space?
Answer 5: For inspiration in designing your garden space, browse home and garden magazines, visit garden centers and showrooms, or explore online resources such as Pinterest and Instagram. Consider the style of your home and the surrounding landscape when making design decisions.

Question 6: How can I make my garden furniture more inviting?
Answer 6: To make your garden furniture more inviting, add comfortable cushions, throw pillows, and blankets. Incorporate outdoor lighting, such as string lights or lanterns, to create a warm and cozy atmosphere. Consider adding potted plants, flowers, and other decorative elements to bring life and color to your garden space.

Tips

Now that you've got a better understanding of garden furniture, let's explore some practical tips to help you make the most of your outdoor space:

Tip 1: Choose the Right Location:
When placing your garden furniture, consider factors such as sun exposure, wind direction, and proximity to your home. Choose a spot that offers a balance of shade and sunlight, and ensure that the furniture is positioned in a way that maximizes comfort and conversation.

Tip 2: Mix and Match:
Don't be afraid to mix and match different styles and materials when choosing your garden furniture. This can create a unique and eclectic look that reflects your personal taste. For example, you could pair a modern metal table with teak chairs or add colorful cushions to a classic wooden bench.

Tip 3: Add Comfort and Style:
Make your garden furniture more inviting by adding comfortable cushions, throw pillows, and blankets. These elements will not only enhance the comfort of your seating but also add a touch of style and personality to your outdoor space.

Tip 4: Incorporate Plants and Accessories:
Surround your garden furniture with potted plants, flowers, and other decorative elements to create a vibrant and inviting atmosphere. Consider adding a water feature, such as a fountain or birdbath, to add a touch of tranquility to your garden.
