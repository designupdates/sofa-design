Another great benefit of outdoor plastic furniture is that it's easy to clean. Unlike wood or metal furniture, plastic furniture doesn't require any special cleaning or maintenance. You can simply wipe it down with a damp cloth and a little soap and water, and it will look good as new.

Here are some tips for cleaning outdoor plastic furniture:
