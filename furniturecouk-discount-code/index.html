Answer 1: Furniture.co.uk offers a wide range of garden furniture, including patio sets, loungers, tables, chairs, and more. You'll find options made from various materials, such as wood, metal, and rattan.

Question 2: How do I choose the right garden furniture for my needs?
Answer 2: Consider the size of your outdoor space, the purpose of the furniture (e.g., dining, lounging, or entertaining), and the style that complements your home and garden.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer 3: Regular cleaning and maintenance will extend the life of your garden furniture. Clean it with mild soap and water, and apply protective treatments as recommended by the manufacturer.

Question 4: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer 4: While some garden furniture is designed to withstand outdoor elements, it's generally recommended to store it indoors or cover it during harsh weather conditions, such as extreme heat, cold, or rain.

Question 5: What are the best materials for garden furniture?
Answer 5: Durable materials like teak, aluminum, and wicker are popular choices for garden furniture as they can withstand outdoor conditions and require minimal maintenance.

Question 6: How do I assemble my garden furniture?
Answer 6: Most garden furniture comes with clear assembly instructions. Follow the steps carefully and use the appropriate tools to ensure proper assembly.
