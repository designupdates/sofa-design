Furniture Sliders for Laminate Floors: A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Nov 23, 2029 Â· 10 min read

Laminate flooring is a popular choice for homes due to its durability, affordability, and ease of installation. However, moving furniture on laminate floors can be a daunting task as it can easily cause scratches and dents. This is where furniture sliders come into play. Furniture sliders are small, disk-shaped devices that are placed underneath furniture legs to allow easy movement without damaging the laminate floor.

Furniture sliders are made from various materials, including plastic, felt, and rubber. Each material has its own unique properties and advantages. Plastic sliders are the most affordable option and are suitable for light to medium-weight furniture. Felt sliders are ideal for heavy furniture as they provide a smooth, non-slip surface. Rubber sliders offer the best protection against scratches and dents and can withstand the weight of even the heaviest furniture.

Now that you understand the basics of furniture sliders, let's explore the different types available and their respective benefits.

Easy installation, removal.

Furniture sliders are very easy to install and remove, making them a convenient option for moving furniture around. To install furniture sliders, simply place them underneath the legs of your furniture. You can then slide your furniture into place without having to lift it up.

To remove furniture sliders, simply lift up the furniture and pull the sliders out from underneath. You can then store the sliders for future use.

Here are some tips for installing and removing furniture sliders:

  • Make sure that the furniture sliders are the right size for your furniture. The sliders should be large enough to support the weight of the furniture, but not so large that they stick out from underneath the furniture.
  • Clean the bottom of your furniture legs before installing the sliders. This will help to ensure that the sliders adhere properly.
  • Be careful not to slide your furniture over rough surfaces, as this could damage the sliders.
  • When removing the sliders, be careful not to pull them out at an angle. This could damage the sliders or the furniture.

Overall, furniture sliders are a very easy-to-use solution for protecting your laminate floors from scratches and dents. They are easy to install and remove, and they can be used on a variety of furniture types.
