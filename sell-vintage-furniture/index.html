How to Sell Vintage Furniture

Written by Petter Sep 27, 2027 · 10 min read

Vintage furniture is a great way to add style and character to your home. It's also a great investment, as many pieces can appreciate in value over time. If you're looking to sell your vintage furniture, there are a few things you'll need to do to get the most out of the process.

First, you'll need to determine the value of your furniture. This can be done by researching comparable pieces online or by consulting with an antique dealer. Once you know how much your furniture is worth, you can start to market it. There are a few different ways to do this, including online marketplaces, print advertising, and even social media.

Once you've found a buyer for your furniture, you'll need to negotiate a price. This can be a bit tricky, so it's important to do your research and know what your furniture is worth. With a little planning and effort, you can sell your vintage furniture for a great price and enjoy it for years to come.

sell vintage furniture

Here are 6 important points about selling vintage furniture:

  • Research the value of your furniture.
  • Market your furniture online and offline.
  • Negotiate a price with buyers.
  • Pack and ship your furniture carefully.
  • Provide excellent customer service.
  • Enjoy the profits!

By following these tips, you can increase your chances of selling your vintage furniture for a good price and having a positive experience overall.

Research the value of your furniture.

Before you can sell your vintage furniture, you need to know how much it's worth. This will help you determine a fair asking price and avoid getting ripped off by potential buyers.

There are a few different ways to research the value of your furniture. One option is to look up comparable pieces online. You can use websites like eBay, Etsy, and Chairish to see what similar items have sold for recently. Another option is to consult with an antique dealer. Antique dealers can provide you with a professional appraisal of your furniture and give you an estimate of its value.

Once you have a good idea of how much your furniture is worth, you can start to market it. There are a few different ways to do this, including online marketplaces, print advertising, and even social media.

When marketing your furniture, be sure to include clear photos and a detailed description. You should also mention the condition of the furniture and any repairs or restoration work that has been done.

By doing your research and marketing your furniture effectively, you can increase your chances of selling it for a good price.

Market your furniture online and offline.

Once you have a good idea of how much your furniture is worth, you can start to market it. There are a few different ways to do this, including online marketplaces, print advertising, and even social media.

**Online marketplaces** are a great way to reach a large audience of potential buyers. There are many different online marketplaces to choose from, including eBay, Etsy, and Chairish. When listing your furniture on an online marketplace, be sure to include clear photos and a detailed description. You should also mention the condition of the furniture and any repairs or restoration work that has been done.

**Print advertising** can also be an effective way to market your furniture, especially if you are targeting a local audience. You can place ads in local newspapers, magazines, and even community newsletters. When placing a print ad, be sure to include a clear photo of your furniture and a brief description. You should also include your contact information so that potential buyers can reach you.

**Social media** is another great way to market your furniture. You can create a dedicated social media page for your furniture business or simply post about your furniture on your personal social media pages. When posting about your furniture on social media, be sure to include clear photos and a detailed description. You should also use relevant hashtags so that potential buyers can find your posts.

By marketing your furniture both online and offline, you can increase your chances of reaching a large audience of potential buyers and selling your furniture for a good price.

Negotiate a price with buyers.

Once you have found a potential buyer for your furniture, you will need to negotiate a price. This can be a bit tricky, so it is important to do your research and know what your furniture is worth. You should also be prepared to compromise, as you are unlikely to get your full asking price.

When negotiating a price, be sure to consider the following factors:

  • The condition of the furniture
  • The rarity of the furniture
  • The demand for the furniture
  • The buyer's budget

Once you have considered all of these factors, you can start to negotiate a price with the buyer. Be prepared to answer questions about the furniture and be willing to compromise. With a little patience and effort, you should be able to reach an agreement with the buyer.

Here are a few tips for negotiating a price with buyers:

  • Be confident and assertive, but be willing to compromise.
  • Be prepared to walk away from the deal if you are not happy with the price.
  • Get everything in writing before you accept payment.

Pack and ship your furniture carefully.

Once you have sold your furniture, you will need to pack and ship it to the buyer. This is a critical step, as you want to ensure that your furniture arrives at its destination in good condition. Here are a few tips for packing and shipping your furniture carefully:

  • Use sturdy boxes and packing materials.
  • Wrap your furniture in bubble wrap or packing paper.
  • Fill empty spaces in the boxes with packing peanuts or crumpled新聞紙.
  • Seal the boxes securely with packing tape.

If you are shipping your furniture via a freight carrier, be sure to get a quote before you book your shipment. Freight carriers typically charge by weight and size, so it is important to have an accurate estimate of the shipping costs before you commit to a carrier.

Once you have packed and shipped your furniture, be sure to provide the buyer with the tracking information so that they can track the shipment and know when to expect delivery.

By following these tips, you can help ensure that your furniture arrives at its destination in good condition and that your buyer is happy with their purchase.

Provide excellent customer service.

Providing excellent customer service is essential to selling vintage furniture. Here are a few tips for providing excellent customer service:

  • Be responsive to inquiries. When potential buyers contact you with questions about your furniture, be sure to respond promptly and professionally. This shows that you are interested in their business and that you are willing to help them find the perfect piece of furniture.
  • Be honest and transparent. When describing your furniture, be sure to be honest and transparent about its condition. Don't try to hide any flaws or imperfections, as this will only lead to disappointment down the road. By being honest and transparent, you can build trust with your customers and increase the chances of them making a purchase.
  • Be willing to negotiate. When negotiating a price with a buyer, be willing to compromise. You are unlikely to get your full asking price, so be prepared to meet the buyer somewhere in the middle. By being willing to negotiate, you can increase the chances of closing the sale and making a happy customer.
  • Follow up with buyers after the sale. After you have sold a piece of furniture, be sure to follow up with the buyer to make sure they are happy with their purchase. This shows that you care about your customers and that you are committed to providing them with a positive experience.

By providing excellent customer service, you can increase your chances of selling your vintage furniture and building a loyal customer base.

Enjoy the profits!

Once you have sold your vintage furniture and provided excellent customer service, it is time to enjoy the profits! You can use the money to buy new furniture, invest in your business, or simply save it for a rainy day.

Selling vintage furniture can be a great way to make extra money and declutter your home. By following the tips in this article, you can increase your chances of selling your furniture for a good price and having a positive experience overall.

So what are you waiting for? Start selling your vintage furniture today!
